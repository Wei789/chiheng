About Chiheng

Chiheng Finance offers focused, distraction-free tools for retirement and asset-allocation planning. We believe good financial decisions come from clear numbers, not screens full of ads and noise. Every calculation runs in your browser; we don't collect or store any figure you enter.

What we offer

GK Dynamic Withdrawal Simulator — model retirement withdrawals year by year with the Guyton-Klinger rules to understand how your savings trend under different market scenarios.

Portfolio Rebalancing Calculator — enter holdings and target allocation to see how to invest new cash or rebalance back to your intended balance.
