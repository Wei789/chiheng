What is rebalancing? Which mode should I use?

As markets move, your portfolio drifts away from its intended allocation. "Rebalancing" brings it back to target to control risk and avoid over-concentration in one asset. This tool offers two approaches:

  • Invest new cash: you have spare cash to deploy. The tool routes it first to the under-weighted holdings, without selling anything — ideal for dollar-cost averaging and saving tax and fees.
  • Full rebalance: gives precise buy and sell suggestions so the allocation lands as close to target as possible — ideal for an annual reset.

FAQ

Q: Must the target percentages add up to 100%?
