Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026.

What we collect

Both tools (the GK Dynamic Withdrawal Simulator and the Portfolio Rebalancing Calculator) are pure front-end tools. Every figure you enter is computed only in your own browser and is never sent to any server — we cannot see it. We require no signup and collect no name, email or financial data.

Browser local storage

If a "save your estimate" feature is added in future, the data will be stored only in your device's browser localStorage and never uploaded. Clearing your browser data removes it.

Third-party services

This site uses Google Fonts; loading fonts causes your browser to request them from Google. This site also uses Google AdSense to display ads: Google and third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can manage or opt out of personalized ads at Google Ads Settings or at aboutads.info. Visitors in the EEA/UK will see a cookie consent notice.

The app (WealthPoise)

The WealthPoise iOS app has its own privacy notice; please refer to its App Store listing and in-app terms.

Your rights & contact

Because these tools collect no personal data, there is currently no account to delete. If you have any privacy questions, you are welcome to contact us.

All tools and content on this site are for education and estimation only and do not constitute investment advice. Investing carries risk; please assess carefully.
